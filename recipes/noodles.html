Garlic Chili Oil Noodles

This 10-minute garlic chili oil noodles is the best quick dinner to whip up on a busy schedule.

Ingredients

  • Chili Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Knife Cut Noodles

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and chop green onions
  2. Boil Knife Cut Noodles for 8 minutes
  3. Create Sauce mixture
